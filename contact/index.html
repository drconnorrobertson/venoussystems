Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can equipment ship?

Most equipment ships within 24–48 hours of insurance approval. Workers' compensation cases with pre-existing authorization often ship the same day.

Will the patient owe anything?

That depends on the insurance plan and the equipment. For workers' compensation and auto/no-fault coverage, patients typically owe nothing. For Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial plans, we verify cost-sharing before shipping and communicate it to the patient in advance.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes — Venous Systems ships to all 50 states. We are an accredited Medicare DMEPOS supplier and credentialed with major commercial and government payers nationwide.

What if the equipment doesn't fit?

We exchange any sized equipment (braces, walkers, sleeves) at no cost if the initial fit is incorrect. A specialist follows up with every patient within one week to verify fit and answer questions.

How do providers set up an ongoing referral relationship?

Call us or submit the form above selecting "Healthcare Provider." We'll assign a dedicated specialist to your practice, set up your preferred order channels, and provide documentation templates for the equipment you most commonly prescribe.
