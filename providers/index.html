How to Refer

  1. Submit the order. Phone, fax, e-prescribe, or our online provider request form — whichever fits your workflow.
  2. We verify benefits. Our team checks coverage and contacts the patient with any cost-sharing information.
  3. We secure authorization. Prior auth, LMN, and supporting documentation handled by our billing team, with your office only involved when a signature is required.
  4. Equipment ships. Direct to the patient, with delivery confirmation back to your office.
  5. Patient is supported. Live phone, video instructions, fit follow-up — all without consuming your clinic's bandwidth.
For high-volume practices: We offer fully integrated workflows — embedded order forms, dedicated fax lines, and EMR-friendly documentation packages. Ask about our practice partner program.

Compliance and Accreditation

Venous Systems is an accredited Medicare DMEPOS supplier, maintains the required surety bonds, and operates under a HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. We do not pay for referrals and operate strictly within Stark Law, Anti-Kickback Statute, and EKRA requirements.
