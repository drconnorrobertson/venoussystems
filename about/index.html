Our Story

Three Decades of Opioid-Free Recovery

Venous Systems was founded on a simple belief: patients heal better, faster, and more completely when their recovery does not depend on pain medication.

How Venous Systems Began

Venous Systems Inc was founded in 1995 in Cleveland, Ohio by a team of medical equipment professionals who saw a gap in how durable medical equipment (DME) was being delivered to recovering patients. Surgeons and physical therapists were prescribing the right devices — thermal compression after knee surgery, intermittent compression to prevent blood clots, TENS units for chronic pain — but the supply chain was slow, the paperwork was painful, and patients were too often falling back on opioids while they waited.

From a small warehouse on the east side of Cleveland, Venous Systems built its model around three principles: ship fast, handle the paperwork, and make sure every patient actually understands how to use the equipment. Thirty years later, those principles still define the company.

The Match One Medical Era

In 2022, Venous Systems was acquired by Match One Medical, a national platform for specialty DME and recovery solutions led by CEO Chaz Jannuzi. The acquisition brought additional infrastructure, expanded payer contracts, and a renewed investment in technology — patient portals, electronic prior authorizations, automated tracking — without disrupting the core mission.

Under Chaz Jannuzi's leadership, Venous Systems has scaled its nationwide reach while doubling down on the same patient-centered service model that made it successful in the first place. The company now ships to all 50 states, supports thousands of referring providers, and remains one of the most trusted names in opioid-free recovery DME.

Our mission: Get the right recovery equipment into the patient's hands quickly, with the paperwork handled, the insurance approved, and the education clear — so they can heal without depending on opioids.

What We Believe About Recovery

The opioid crisis is the most important public health story of our generation, and DME has a real role to play. Every product we ship is selected because it addresses pain or dysfunction through physiology — circulation, swelling, muscle re-engagement, mechanical stability — not pharmacology.

  • Move the blood. Compression therapy reduces swelling and prevents clots.
  • Re-engage the muscle. NMES restores firing patterns after injury.
  • Modulate pain at the nerve. TENS therapy uses electrical stimulation instead of medication.
  • Stabilize the structure. Bracing protects healing tissue.
  • Restore mobility. Walkers and mobility aids get the patient up and moving.

Who We Serve

Venous Systems supplies equipment to a wide range of provider types and patient situations:

  • Orthopedic surgeons managing post-operative knee, hip, and shoulder recovery
  • Vascular and general surgeons concerned with post-op DVT risk
  • Pain management physicians prescribing TENS and NMES for chronic conditions
  • Workers' compensation case managers coordinating return-to-work plans
  • Physical therapists, chiropractors, and rehabilitation clinics
  • Hospital discharge planners and home health agencies

Where We Are Today

Three decades in, Venous Systems has grown into a national operation while preserving the responsiveness of a regional supplier. We're proud of our roots in Cleveland and proud to be part of the Match One Medical family. Most of all, we're proud of the patients we've helped recover — without opioids, without delays, and without leaving providers to fight the paperwork alone.
