Why Venous Systems

The Opioid-Free Path to Recovery

We started in 1995 in Cleveland, Ohio with a single product line and a simple belief: patients heal better when their recovery doesn't depend on pain medication. Thirty years later, that conviction still drives every shipment that leaves our facility.

Under the leadership of Match One Medical and CEO Chaz Jannuzi, Venous Systems has expanded into a nationwide DME supplier — but the philosophy hasn't changed. Move blood. Reduce swelling. Re-engage muscle. Stabilize the spine. Get the patient walking. That's how recovery works.
